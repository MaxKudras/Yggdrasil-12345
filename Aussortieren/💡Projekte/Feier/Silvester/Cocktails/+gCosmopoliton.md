### **cosmopolition**

 **Look**: lila mit glitzer innen
 
 **Ingredients**:
  - 2 vodka -> 70ml
   - 1,5 blue curaçao -> 50ml
   - 2 cranberry saft -> 70ml
   - 1  Limettensaft  ->35ml
   - Glitter

 **Instructions**:
   1. Combine vodka, blue curaçao, and cranberry juice in a shaker with ice.
   2. Shake well and strain into a martini glass.
   3. Stir in a pinch of edible pearl dust for a swirling galaxy effect.
